Our Story

Sweet Treats Candy Store was founded in 2010 by a group of friends who shared a common love for sweets. The idea was simple: to create a haven for candy enthusiasts of all ages. Starting from a small corner shop, our store has grown over the years, becoming a beloved part of the community.

Our Selection

We take immense pride in our extensive and diverse selection of candies from around the world. From traditional classics to modern favorites, we aim to cater to every taste bud. You can find a wide array of:

  • Chocolates: Rich, creamy, and indulgent, we offer a variety of chocolate bars, truffles, and pralines sourced from renowned chocolatiers.
  • Gummies and Jellies: For the gummy candy lovers, we have a rainbow of gummies and jellies in various shapes and flavors, from sour to sweet.
  • Hard Candies: Discover the old-fashioned pleasure of sucking on hard candies in various flavors, perfect for nostalgic moments.
  • Licorice: For licorice enthusiasts, we have an assortment of black, red, and flavored licorice to satisfy your cravings.
  • Sour Candies: If you enjoy a tangy twist, our collection of sour candies will leave your taste buds tingling with delight.
  • International Delights: Explore candies from different countries and experience the cultural diversity of confections.

Our Values

At Sweet Treats Candy Store, we prioritize quality, variety, and exceptional customer service. We carefully handpick each candy, ensuring that they meet our stringent quality standards. Our team is always ready to assist you in finding the perfect candy for your taste or helping you choose a thoughtful gift.

Community Engagement

As a part of the community, we strongly believe in giving back. Throughout the year, we organize charity events and collaborate with local schools and organizations to spread joy and sweetness to those in need.

Visit Us

If you are a candy aficionado or just looking for a sweet escape, we invite you to visit Sweet Treats Candy Store. Our vibrant and colorful store is designed to evoke feelings of nostalgia and happiness. Whether you are young or young at heart, there's something for everyone to cherish and enjoy.
